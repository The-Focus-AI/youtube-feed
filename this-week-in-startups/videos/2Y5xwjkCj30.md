# Anthropic wants to slow AI down and Bernie wants 50%: JCal Reacts | E2297

## Summary

The cold open is a triple-shot: Anthropic published a blog post calling for a global AI slowdown, Bernie Sanders dropped a video proposing the government seize 50% of major AI companies' stock through the "American AI Sovereign Wealth Fund Act," and Jason predicts the 2028 presidential election will turn on AI job-displacement policy. The discussion winds through every angle — why Anthropic's timing is bizarre (they're on the verge of an IPO), why Bernie's pitch will land with both MAGA and the left, and Jason's pitched grand bargain: collapse SNAP/Medicaid/housing programs into a flat-rate-tax UBI funded partly by AI lab equity stakes.

The episode opens with a live ComfyUI demo from co-founder Yoland Yan, walking through node-based generative-AI workflows: an Ideogram V4 image generation with bounding boxes, outpainting Jason from a TikTok format for the Vegas Sphere Wizard of Oz show, and turning Jason into a Simpsons character. ComfyUI just raised $30M at $500M post from Craft Ventures (David Sacks). Jason gives Yoland his "build sales in Phoenix/Vegas, not SF" speech, name-checking Tony Hsieh and Zappos. Plus segments on Brian Chesky's new AI lab, LA mayoral Polymarket odds (Spencer Pratt at 4% vs. Karen Bass at 66%), the rediscovered TWiST portfolio company Roam Around (a 2023 travel agent that pre-dated Chesky's idea), Mandalorian and Grogu, the Smile horror box-office model, Alex Honnold's Netflix free climb, and Pump.fun's new "GO" bounty product.
