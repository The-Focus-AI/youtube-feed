# This Decentralized Cloud is Thousands of Times Cheaper Than AWS | EP 2262

## Summary

This episode covers two wildly different startup stories. First, Mog and Dubs from Subnet 75 (Hippius) explain how they are using the Bittensor blockchain to provide decentralized cloud storage that is dramatically cheaper than AWS and other hyperscalers. By incentivizing miners to contribute storage space with TAO tokens, Hippius creates a marketplace for compute and storage that operates without centralized control, with hundreds of contributors already participating across the network.

The second half features an entertaining interview with Charlie Hurst, Tom Noble, and Will Sudlow, the founders of "Flat White or F**k Off" -- a brand that started as a joke by legendary British ad man Rory Sutherland on a podcast and was turned into a real business by these three entrepreneurs. From viral social media accounts to a line of merchandise to a successful London pop-up cafe, they demonstrate the power of experiential marketing and the "Rule of 3s" in hospitality. The episode closes with Jason and co-host Lon Harris discussing their Oscar picks and Lon's experience going "Inside the Manosphere."
