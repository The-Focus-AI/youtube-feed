# Why You'll Choose an AI Doctor (feat. Fred Almeida and Max Weiss) | E2239

## Summary

Recorded in Tokyo during Founder University's Japan expansion, this episode features Jason Calacanis in conversation with Fred Almeida (founder and CEO of American Medical Intelligence Inc.) and Maxwell Weiss (partner at Pacific Bays Capital). The discussion centers on the future of AI in medicine, with Fred arguing that AI will soon serve as your general practitioner -- diagnosing conditions, recommending specialists, and enabling individuals to take greater control over preventative care strategies. This vision represents a fundamental shift from reactive to proactive healthcare.

The episode also provides a fascinating window into Japan's technology landscape. Max explains how Japan has stayed at the forefront of technology despite not being a traditional manufacturing hub, producing the underlying tech behind many global products. The panel discusses "genten shugi" -- a Japanese cultural concept of perfectionism that ironically prevents some Japanese people from communicating in English despite understanding the language. The conversation reveals that many Americans are now willing to take pay cuts to work in Japan, drawn by quality of life, safety, and culture. The episode concludes with five startup pitches from Founder University x Japan companies, with Jason, Fred, and Max providing real-time feedback on Cascade, Altsource Global, GoalMochi, RandomChat, and Eir.
