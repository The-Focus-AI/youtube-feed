# Aboard Podcast Channel Index

The Aboard Podcast is hosted by Paul Ford and Rich Ziade, co-founders of Aboard (formerly Postlight). The show covers technology, AI, business, and culture from a practical, often humorous perspective -- featuring sharp analysis, corporate roleplays, and conversations with journalists, engineers, and industry leaders navigating the AI transformation.
