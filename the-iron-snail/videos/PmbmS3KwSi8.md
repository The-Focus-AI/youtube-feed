# The TRUTH About Merino Wool Shirts.

## Summary

Michael puts merino wool shirts to the test against synthetic and cotton alternatives, addressing internet horror stories about holes, durability issues, and environmental concerns. Using over 20 sources and conducting his own smell tests, drying tests, and strength tests, he delivers a data-driven verdict on whether merino lives up to the hype.

The video compares Unbound Merino, Patagonia Capilene Cool (synthetic with mint oils), Proof 72-Hour (synthetic), and standard cotton. Key findings: merino dramatically outperforms synthetic on odor resistance, dries slower than synthetics but faster than cotton, and has genuine durability concerns that can be mitigated with proper care. The controversial ending addresses superwash treatment - the process that makes merino washable also makes it less sustainable than marketing suggests.

Michael ultimately concludes merino is worth it for specific use cases (travel, multi-day wear) but isn't a magic fabric. The truth is nuanced: great for smell, mediocre for durability, complicated environmentally.
