# Reviewing Everything on my Desk! (2026)

## Summary

Marques Brownlee reviews every piece of equipment on his desk, many of which he has used for years or even over a decade. Unlike typical reviews where he spends days or weeks with a product, this video focuses on gear he knows intimately from years of daily use. He scores each item and provides honest assessments of long-term ownership, including what he plans to replace soon.

The standout items include the XDesk Air Pro (formerly Next Desk), a massive 8-foot aluminum sit-stand desk he's had for nearly a decade and calls his "forever desk" (9/10), and the Herman Miller Embody chair which gets a perfect 10/10 for long-term comfort. The most surprising revelation is his Mac Pro rating -- just 4/10 -- as the $18,000 M2 Ultra tower has been abandoned by Apple through three chip generations with no update in sight. He plans to replace it with an M5 Ultra Mac Studio. The dual Pro Display XDRs ($7,000 each with stand and nano-texture) get an 8/10 despite being 60Hz panels with no webcam or speakers.

On the peripherals side, Brownlee reveals a quirky workflow: he uses both a mouse and a Magic Trackpad simultaneously for Final Cut Pro editing, with the trackpad handling timeline scrolling and gestures while the MX Master 4 mouse handles everything else. The Rainy 75 Lite mechanical keyboard, Yamaha HS8 monitors, and Sennheiser HD 650 headphones round out a setup where familiarity and long-term reliability trump cutting-edge specifications.
