# Samsung Galaxy Buds 4 Pro Review: Better than AirPods!

## Summary

MKBHD reviews Samsung's new Galaxy Buds 4 Pro, which launched quietly alongside the Galaxy S26 lineup. He cuts straight to the point: these are incredible earbuds that he considers better than AirPods Pro. At the same $250 price point, they match or exceed Apple's offering in nearly every category -- active noise cancellation, transparency mode, sound quality, design, and customization -- while adding features AirPods lack like a full nine-band equalizer, gaming mode, and multiple color options.

The review covers the redesigned cube case with a tinted clear lid, the new square-stemmed bud design with brushed metal accents, and the dual-driver sound system with separate amp for each driver. Marques highlights that the ANC at maximum strength is as strong or stronger than AirPods Pro, and the ambient mode is nearly as good as Apple's transparency mode. Smart features like automatic siren detection and voice-activated ambient switching round out the package.

Notably, MKBHD tests the buds paired with an iPhone, confirming they work as basic Bluetooth earbuds but lose Samsung's customization features. He argues the only reason these cannot replace AirPods on iPhone is because Apple restricts third-party integration. For Android users, he declares these his favorite wireless earbuds of 2026 and "sneaky winners" that flew under the radar during Samsung's S26 launch.
