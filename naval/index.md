### The Deutsch Files Series

A four-part long-form interview series with physicist David Deutsch, joined by Brett Hall. Together the conversations span Deutsch's "four strands of reality" (quantum physics, computation, evolution, epistemology), Constructor Theory, the multiverse and quantum computing, anti-rational memes, Taking Children Seriously, the Enlightenment, and Western civilization's capacity for error correction. Watching them in order is the closest thing to a guided tour of *The Fabric of Reality* and *The Beginning of Infinity* with the authors as guides.
