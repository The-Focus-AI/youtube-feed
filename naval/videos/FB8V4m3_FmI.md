# You Have to Enjoy It a Lot

## Summary

Naval frames marketing as an open problem with no single right answer — videos, writing, tweeting, parties, even a sandwich board can all work. The real question is which of these you genuinely enjoy doing, because the medium that wins for you is the one you'll keep doing long after most people quit. He warns friends who pitch him on starting a podcast for marketing reasons: "Do you genuinely enjoy talking? Do you genuinely enjoy talking a lot?" If not, listeners will pick up on the flatness within a few episodes and the project dies.

His prototype is Joe Rogan, who was so immersed in talking to weird people that "the guy would be doing it even if he had no audience. And he was doing it when he had no audience" — late-night Ustream, no one watching. The conclusion: marketing, business, and role are all a *fit problem*, a *matching problem*. Lean into specific knowledge, pick the medium that's natural (Substack for long-form, X for short-form, video if you like making video), pick a business or co-founder where your natural mode lines up, and try everything until you hone in on what you'd do for free.

## Key Points

- **Marketing is an open problem** ([0:00](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=FB8V4m3_FmI&t=0s)) - Videos, writing, tweets, parties, sandwich boards — all can work
- **Pick a business congruent with the marketing you enjoy** ([0:00](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=FB8V4m3_FmI&t=0s)) - Most important alignment
- **The "do you enjoy talking?" test for podcasts** ([0:30](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=FB8V4m3_FmI&t=30s)) - Asked of friends who want to start one for marketing
- **Listeners detect insincerity within a few episodes** ([0:55](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=FB8V4m3_FmI&t=55s)) - "Doing the podcast equivalent of looking at their watch"
- **Pick the medium that matches your form** ([1:31](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=FB8V4m3_FmI&t=91s)) - Substack for long-form, X for short-form, podcasts/Spaces for talkers, AI tools for video
- **Or pick a co-founder** ([2:18](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=FB8V4m3_FmI&t=138s)) - Fit can be solved by team composition
- **Unlimited venues, unlimited media** ([2:18](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=FB8V4m3_FmI&t=138s)) - The modern world's gift to fit
- **Be in the arena** ([2:18](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=FB8V4m3_FmI&t=138s)) - The first attempts will be wrong; that's how you find the right one
