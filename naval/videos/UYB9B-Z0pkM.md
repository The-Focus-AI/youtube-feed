# Hiring a Podcast Editor and Naval's Chief of Staff

## Summary

Nivi announces two job openings around Naval Ravikant. The first is a part-time editor for the Naval podcast — described (with characteristic understatement) as "the most timeless and overproduced podcast in human history." The editor will work primarily in Descript, edit transcripts for clarity, and post videos to social media. The bar is high in temperament rather than experience: "DSM-5 level attention to detail," strong writing, a nose for design, and the ability to "get things the first time you hear them."

The second role is Naval's personal chief of staff — a full-time, San Francisco-based, technical role that runs the gamut "from prestigious to pauperian": traveling the world to recruit engineers, researching investments, throwing events, and handling personal tasks. Both roles share the same application format: under 750 characters to `podcast@nav.al` or `chief@nav.al`, with links to your best work, your smartest tweet, a hard problem you've solved, new knowledge you're creating for fun, and something you've made with AI. Shorter is better.
