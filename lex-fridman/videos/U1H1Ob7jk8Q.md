# Jack Weatherford: Genghis Khan and the Mongol Empire

## Summary

Jack Weatherford is an anthropologist and historian who has written legendary books on Genghis Khan and the Mongol Empire. The conversation traces Genghis Khan's journey from a 9-year-old boy named Temujin who lost everything - his father, his tribe - to become the conqueror of the largest contiguous empire in history. They discuss his childhood on the Mongolian steppe, his revolutionary military tactics, religious freedom in the empire, trade on the Silk Road, and his genetic legacy that lives on in millions of people today.
