# Dave Plummer: Programming, Autism, and Old-School Microsoft Stories

## Summary

Dave Plummer is a programmer, former Microsoft software engineer (Windows 95, NT, XP), creator of Windows Task Manager, author of two books on autism, and host of the Dave's Garage YouTube channel. In this conversation, Dave shares stories from his decades at Microsoft, including creating Task Manager, the ZIP compression support, and even Space Cadet Pinball. They discuss debugging, the Blue Screen of Death, who the best programmers are, and the future of programming.
