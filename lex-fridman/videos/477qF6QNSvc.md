# Tim Sweeney: Fortnite, Unreal Engine, and the Future of Gaming

## Summary

Tim Sweeney is a legendary video game programmer, founder and CEO of Epic Games. He created Unreal Engine, Fortnite, Gears of War, and Unreal Tournament. The conversation covers his 10,000+ hours of programming practice, the origin story of Epic Games, how Unreal Engine evolved from a scrappy game engine to Hollywood's tool of choice, rendering realistic humans, Lumen global illumination, the metaverse vision, Fortnite's massive scale (100 million players), the Verse programming language, and his epic battle with Apple over app store monopoly.
