# Sundar Pichai: CEO of Google and Alphabet

## Summary

Sundar Pichai is the CEO of Google and Alphabet. In this conversation, he shares his journey from India (waiting 5 years for a telephone, fetching water during droughts) to leading one of the most powerful companies in the world. They discuss AI's impact on human history, Veo 3 and video generation, scaling laws, AGI and ASI timelines, p(doom), the toughest leadership decisions during criticism, AI mode vs traditional Google Search, Chrome and the DOJ case, Android, and demos of Google Beam and XR Glasses.
