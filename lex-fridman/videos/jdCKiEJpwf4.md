# Scott Horton: The Case Against War and the Military Industrial Complex

## Summary

Scott Horton is the director of the Libertarian Institute, editorial director of Antiwar.com, and for three decades a staunch critic of US military interventionism. This epic 10+ hour conversation covers the entire history of US foreign policy from the Cold War to the War on Terror, examining the devastating costs: 900,000+ direct deaths, 3.6 million indirect deaths, and $8 trillion spent. Horton argues that nearly every US military intervention has failed to accomplish its stated objectives while creating blowback that leads to more conflict.
