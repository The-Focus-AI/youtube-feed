# Robert Rodriguez: Sin City, Desperado, El Mariachi, Alita, and Filmmaking

## Summary

Robert Rodriguez is a legendary filmmaker who created Sin City, El Mariachi, Desperado, Spy Kids, Machete, From Dusk Till Dawn, and Alita: Battle Angel. The conversation covers his scrappy origins making El Mariachi for $7,000, how creative constraints breed innovation, his collaborations with Quentin Tarantino and James Cameron, working with Salma Hayek and Danny Trejo, the technical artistry of Sin City (making the comic move), sound design, editing, deadlines as creative fuel, and the philosophy of manifesting your dreams through relentless work.
