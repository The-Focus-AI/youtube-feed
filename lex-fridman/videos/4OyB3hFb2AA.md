# Oliver Anthony: Country Music, Blue-Collar America, Fame, Money, and Pain

## Summary

Oliver Anthony (real name Christopher Anthony Lunsford) is a singer-songwriter from Virginia who gained worldwide fame with "Rich Men North of Richmond." He became a voice for the voiceless, speaking to the struggles of the working class. Lex met him at an open mic, watching him give everything to a small audience. The conversation covers mainstream country music, handling sudden fame, the politics that tried to co-opt his music, depression, finding peace in nature, and includes a live performance of "I Want to Go Home."
