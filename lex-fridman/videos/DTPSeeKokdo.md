# Ezra Klein and Derek Thompson: Politics, Trump, AOC, Elon & DOGE

## Summary

Ezra Klein (NY Times, The Ezra Klein Show) and Derek Thompson (The Atlantic, Plain English podcast) discuss their book "Abundance" and the future of progressive politics. They argue that Democrats need to embrace building things - housing, energy, infrastructure - rather than just redistributing wealth. They discuss why government is often inefficient (and how to fix it), DOGE and Elon Musk, AOC as a potential leader, the political realignment happening in America, and why the left needs to be willing to upset people to change the party.
