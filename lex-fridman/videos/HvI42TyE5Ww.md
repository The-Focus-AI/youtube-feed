# Douglas Murray: Putin, Zelenskyy, Trump, Israel, Netanyahu, Hamas & Gaza

## Summary

Douglas Murray is the author of "On Democracies and Death Cults," "The War on The West," and "The Madness of Crowds." In this conversation, he defends Israel's actions in Gaza, criticizes Western responses to both the Ukraine war and the Israel-Hamas conflict, discusses Putin's ambitions, Zelenskyy's leadership, Netanyahu's governance, and the nature of evil. Murray has visited Gaza and Israel and speaks from direct experience. He represents one perspective on these controversial issues; Lex notes he will also interview people with different views.
