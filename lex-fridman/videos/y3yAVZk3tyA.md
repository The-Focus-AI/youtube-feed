# Keyu Jin: China's Economy, Tariffs, Trade, Trump, Communism & Capitalism

## Summary

Keyu Jin is an economist at the London School of Economics specializing in China's economy. She wrote "The New China Playbook: Beyond Socialism and Capitalism" which dispels many Western misconceptions about China. In this conversation, she explains that China's economy is actually more decentralized than the US (the "Mayor Economy"), discusses the nuanced relationship between Chinese people and authority, and addresses DeepSeek, tariffs, Taiwan, Jack Ma, and whether China's economy is about to collapse.
